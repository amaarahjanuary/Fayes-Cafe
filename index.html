Faye's Cafe

At Faye's Cafe we sell a variety of savoury and sweet treats.

We also cater for small to medium sized events.

Savouries

Halfmoons

These are nice appetizers in the shape of a halfmoon. They're crispy outside with a creamy chicken filling.

Samoosas

A samoosa is a fried pastry with a savory filling like spiced potatoes, onions, chicken and other meats.

Springrolls

A springroll is a pancake filled with vegetables and sometimes meat, rolled into a cylinder and fried.



Pies

Homemade hand pies with a savory filling, wrapped in a buttery, flaky crust. It is baked to golden perfection.

Pizzas

Mini pizzas topped with sauce and toppings and baked until dough is cooked and cheese is melty.

See ingredients
Quiche

A tart consisting of pastry crust filled with savoury custard and pieces of cheese, meat, seafood or vegetables.

See ingredients

Sweet Treats

Koeksisters

A koeksister is a traditional Afrikaner confectionery made of fried dough infused in syrup or honey.

See ingredients
Snowballs

A cake like treat, dipped in jam and coated in dessicated coconut. Cream can also be added as a filling.

See ingredients
Koesisters

Koesisters are fried balls of dough dipped in syrup and optionally topped with dessicated coconut.

See ingredients

Meals

Soup

Vegetable soup is a hearty soup prepared using vegetables and leaf vegetables as primary ingredients.

See ingredients
Salomie

A rich, spicy curry of your choice wrapped in a thick, flaky roti. It is a very popular filling meal.

Breyani

Biryani is a mixed rice dish made with Indian spices, rice, and meat or chicken and potatoes.
